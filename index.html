Be Human .IO

A differing ideology to how we use technology.

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Our Primary Directive

  • Be Human: don’t act like a machine to society, acknowledge them as we did pre-telephone.
  • IO: To perform an input or output operation between human and machine; electronics are always used.

BeHuman.IO is a series of projects, experiments and exportation into the unknown, all under one common goal: for the betterment of society. Be them open source, free or paid, all their underlying use is to improve and enhance ourselves. Mind and body.

Current Projects

  • Be More .Clothing: Philadelphia's first independent fashionable wearables boutique.

Past Projects

  • Interpersonal Campus Security: An start to end system to watch and protect students as they to and from school.
  • Philly Pack Walk: A service design to assist dog owner’s have their pet[s] walked when they are unable too because of work or other busy reasons.
  • In My Shoes: A virtual reality computer simulation platform designed to build empathy on an array of disorders & diseases by experiencing the another's world for a brief moment in time. You'll see the world as they do and internalize how they feel.
  • Augmented Reality World Reactive System: Hearing, Visual & Mental user adaptive life Assistive device
